<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What Goes Into Residential to Commercial Conversion?</h2>



<p>Converting a commercial space into residential units involves tons of interior renovation, but it also requires an understanding of zoning laws and building codes tailored for residential occupancy. Before you transform a building for residential use, be aware of the existing zoning regulations. You&#8217;ll need to research your local municipality, since ordinances vary.</p>



<p>Residential building codes are significantly different from commercial ones, so renovating an existing structure may present unexpected costs. Commercial buildings often have different utility setups. Overhauling a site&#8217;s plumbing, HVAC, or electricity can be intensive, costly work.</p>
