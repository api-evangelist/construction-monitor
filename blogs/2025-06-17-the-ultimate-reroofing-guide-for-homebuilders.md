<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What is the first step to reroofing a home?</h2>



<p>Before thinking about material choices, you must secure the necessary permits.&nbsp;<a href="https://na.hubexo.com/permit-holder-data-and-insights-review/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Permit</a>&nbsp;requirements vary by state and sometimes down to the local municipality. Permits ensure your project adhere to local building codes. This is a major selling point to homeowners in the future and it protects your liability. Researching and applying for permits can be time-consuming. Missing deadlines and submitting incomplete applications can lead to costly delays, so be proactive!</p>
